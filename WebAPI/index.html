By default, the suggestions will be updated to those returned by the bot. To have the bot suggestions automatically played, add &botPlay=true to the end of the URL.

We log console messages regarding bot suggestions and indicate when they differ. To see these messages, press F12 to open the browser dev tools, go to the Console tab, and enter "Bot suggested" in the Filter field.
